Privacy Policy

Chronoglyph  ·  Effective 28 May 2026  ·  Last updated 28 May 2026

Short version: Chronoglyph stores your glyphs, photos, videos, and audio entirely on your device. We do not collect personal data, run analytics on your usage, or share anything with third parties. Your memories stay on your phone.

1. Who we are

Chronoglyph is a mobile application developed and published by Novaforge Labs. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at benjaminliamharris@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

We do not collect personal information. Chronoglyph requires no account, no sign-up, and no email address to use. All data you create in the app — glyphs, photos, videos, audio recordings, and location lock coordinates — is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

The following device permissions are requested solely to operate core app features:

  • Location (foreground and background): Used to capture GPS coordinates when you create a glyph, and to monitor your position in the background so the app can automatically unlock a glyph when you arrive at the specified location. Location data is stored on-device only and never uploaded.
  • Camera: Used to capture photos and videos to include in your glyphs. Media is saved locally to the app's secure storage.
  • Microphone: Used to record audio messages for your glyphs. Audio files are saved locally.
  • Photo library: Used to save or retrieve media from your device's photo library when you choose to. The app does not scan or read your photo library without your action.
  • Face ID / Touch ID / Biometric authentication: Used to lock access to your private glyphs. Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system (iOS Secure Enclave / Android Keystore) and is never accessible to or stored by the app.

3. Optional cloud backup

Chronoglyph Premium includes an optional encrypted cloud backup feature. If you enable it:

  • Your glyph data is encrypted on-device before upload using keys that only you hold.
  • We cannot read the content of your backups.
  • You can delete your cloud backup at any time from Settings.
  • You can use Chronoglyph without ever enabling cloud backup.

4. Crash and diagnostic data

The app may collect anonymous crash reports via the platform's standard crash reporting tools (Apple's built-in crash reporter on iOS; Android Vitals on Android). These reports contain only technical stack trace information. They do not include personal data, location coordinates, glyph content, or any media you have created. You can opt out via your device's system privacy settings.

5. Children

Chronoglyph is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will provide guidance on deleting any locally stored data from the device.

6. Data retention and deletion

Because all data is stored on your device, you control retention entirely. You can delete individual glyphs within the app, or delete all app data by uninstalling Chronoglyph. If you have enabled cloud backup, you can delete your cloud backup separately from Settings > Account > Delete Backup.

7. Third-party services

Chronoglyph does not integrate third-party advertising, analytics SDKs, or social sign-on services. The app does not contain ads.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will provide notice within the app. Continued use of Chronoglyph after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
